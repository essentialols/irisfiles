Frequently Asked Questions

Most online converters upload your files to their servers for processing. IrisFiles runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly technology. Your images never leave your device, making it the most private way to convert images online. In March 2025, the FBI warned about malicious online file converters that install malware. With IrisFiles, there's nothing to install and no server to trust.

Yes, completely free with no account required and no watermarks. There is a 100 MB per file limit and a 100-megapixel image limit to keep things running smoothly in your browser. IrisFiles costs almost nothing to run (static files, no server processing), so there's no reason to charge.

IrisFiles supports images (HEIC, WebP, PNG, JPG, GIF, BMP, SVG, AVIF, TIFF, ICO), audio (MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A, AAC), documents (EPUB, RTF, DOCX), fonts (TTF, OTF, WOFF), PDFs, video to GIF, and ZIP archives. All conversions happen in your browser.
