Frequently Asked Questions

IrisFiles re-encodes your image at a lower quality setting using your browser's Canvas API. For JPG and WebP, this means more aggressive lossy compression. Lower quality means smaller files but more visible artifacts.

For photos shared online or via email, 75-85% offers a good balance. For archival or print, stay at 92% or above. Below 60%, compression artifacts become noticeable in most photos.
