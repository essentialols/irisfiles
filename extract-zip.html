Frequently Asked Questions

IrisFiles uses fflate, a fast JavaScript ZIP library, to decompress your archive entirely in the browser. No server upload is needed.

There is no hard limit, but very large archives may be slow depending on your device memory. Most archives under 100MB extract quickly.

Currently only ZIP format is supported. RAR, 7z, and tar.gz are not yet available.
