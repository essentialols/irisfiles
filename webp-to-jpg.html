Frequently Asked Questions

WebP is a modern format created by Google that's widely used on the web. However, some older software, email clients, and social media platforms don't support WebP. Converting to JPG ensures your images work everywhere.

Both WebP and JPG are lossy formats, so there is a small quality loss during conversion. At the default 92% quality setting, the difference is imperceptible for most photos. You can increase it to 100% if quality is critical.
