Frequently Asked Questions

AVIF (AV1 Image Format) is a modern image format based on the AV1 video codec. It offers excellent compression, often better than WebP and JPG, but isn't supported by all software yet.

JPG is universally supported by every device, browser, and application. Converting AVIF to JPG ensures maximum compatibility when sharing images.

Chrome 85+, Firefox 93+, and Safari 16.1+ can decode AVIF natively. This converter uses your browser's built-in AVIF decoder.
