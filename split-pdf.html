Frequently Asked Questions

IrisFiles uses pdf-lib, a client-side JavaScript library, to read your PDF and extract each page into its own separate PDF file. The entire process runs in your browser with no server involvement.

If the PDF has an owner password (restricting editing but allowing viewing), IrisFiles can usually split it. If it requires a user password to open, you will need to unlock it first.

There is no hard page limit. Since everything runs in your browser, the practical limit depends on your device's available memory. Most devices can handle PDFs with hundreds of pages without issues.
