GIF supports binary transparency (fully transparent or fully opaque pixels). Semi-transparent areas in PNG will be flattened, but fully transparent regions are preserved.

GIF supports a maximum of 256 colors. PNGs with many colors (like photos) will lose some detail. For graphics, logos, and illustrations with limited colors, the result is nearly identical.
