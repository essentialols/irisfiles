Frequently Asked Questions

MOV is Apple's native video format, required by Final Cut Pro, iMovie, and other Apple editing tools. If you have AVI files that need to be edited or played on macOS or iOS, converting to MOV ensures seamless compatibility with Apple's ecosystem.

IrisFiles uses FFmpeg.wasm, a WebAssembly port of FFmpeg. It decodes the video and audio from your AVI file and re-encodes them into a QuickTime MOV container. All processing runs in your browser with no server involvement.

Files must be under 100MB and 10 minutes. AVI files are often large due to minimal compression, so check your file size first. For bigger files, use HandBrake or Apple Compressor.

Yes. Your files never leave your device. The FFmpeg.wasm library is downloaded once from a CDN and cached. All conversion happens in your browser's memory.
